## What is YouTube-Floater?

YouTube-Floater is a standalone macOS application that enables you to play YouTube videos in a floating, always-on-top window while working on other tasks. Perfect for MacBook users with a single screen who want to watch videos without switching between windows.

## How It Works

YouTube-Floater provides a seamless viewing experience with these core capabilities:

1. **Paste & Play** - Simply paste any YouTube URL and the video instantly embeds in the floating window
2. **Transparency Control** - Adjust window opacity to see your work underneath while watching
3. **Volume Management** - Independent volume control that doesn't affect other system audio
4. **Toggleable Controls** - Hide the control panel to minimize distractions when not needed
5. **Browser Integration** - Quick option to open the video in your default browser
